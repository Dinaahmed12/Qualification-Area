about us

Work is the first means by which every person can obtain money, and then have his needs and the needs of his family satisfied. In fact, in light of the special circumstances in our Arab region of not having enough jobs, and the lack of a real link between education and the labor market, most young people are living in a state of confusion to get the right job. This site idea will be dedicated to helping young graduates of BIS College to reach the most in-demand fields of work, and to help them qualify for the most important types of jobs..

(Training) : Training is your way to test yourself in a practical and dynamic work environment or moving and changing! (Courses): In the fields offered by the college, such as: 1- The field of information systems, which is one of the branches of information technology, and it includes programming and computer science courses of all kinds. 2- The field of data analysis or data science, and this is the science of mathematics and statistics, and it is useful for all data science courses of all kinds, but it is a purely mathematical field that depends on algebra and statistics 3- The field of business administration, and this is useful for management courses of all kinds, the best of which are resource management courses Human HR.
